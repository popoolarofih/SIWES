PUFF PUFF

1hr 30 mins | 500 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • cups of flour
  • cup of water and cup of sugar
  • teaspoon of yeast
  • teaspoon of Nutmeg
  • salt to taste
  • Enough oil for deep frying

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Mix half of a cup of warm water, 1 teaspoon of sugar and the yeast in a bowl. Cover with a lid and set asid until the yeast becomes bubbly. 4-6 mins
  • STEP 2 In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients - flour, sugar, salt and nutmeg.
  • STEP 3 Pour the proofed yeast and the remaining one cup of water together with the dry ingredients and mix until the batter is lump free
  • STEP 4 Cover the dough and allow it to rise for 1 hour. the risen flour has a distinct sweet smell, that will make you know the dough is ready for frying.
  • STEP 5 Preheat the oil in a pan deep and wide enough to accomodate the puff puff balls and scoop a little bit of dough and drop the batter by spoonful into the oil.
  • STEP 6Fry both sides until golden brown. If you are finding it hard to turn the puff puff while frying you can just press down the balls a little bit with your frying spoon before turning.
  • STEP 7remove from heat. Drain on paper towel and enjoy while hot or warm
  • STEP 8Use a slotted spoon and transfer the buns on a paper towel.
  • STEP 9Sweeten to taste with sugar, refrigerate and serve chilled.

DOUGHNUT

1hr | 700 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • cups of flour
  • tablespoon of Bakers yeast
  • cup of milk, milk cup sugar, egg, whisked
  • salt to taste
  • cups of groundnut for deep frying

METHOD

  • STEP 1 In a small bowl, pour the sugar into the milk and mix with a spoon to dissolve.
  • STEP 2 In a medium sized empty bowl, pour about two cups of flour, yeast, salt and mix them together. Make a well in the center and gently pour in the milk/sugar mixture and mix with a spatula. Rinse the milk bowl with about 1 cup of water and add to the batter then mix gently to make a semi thick paste.
  • STEP 3 Add the melted butter and whisked egg into the batter and mix to incorporate.
  • STEP 4 Gently fold in the rest of the flour till you have a strechy dough. Ditch the spatula, wash your hands, bring out the dough with slean hands. If the dough feels sticky, add some flour and knead some more. The dough should have a non sticky, stecthy feel like chewed gum.
  • STEP 5 Once you've achieved the right feel, roll the dough into a ball with your hands and place in the bowl it was mixed in. Cover with a cloth and leave to stand for about 30 mins
  • STEP 6By the time you open up the cloth, the dough would have risen and doubled in size. Take it out, and knead again.
  • STEP 7Flour the work surface and rolling pin then roll out dough till its about 1/3 the height of your pinky finger(last finger).
  • STEP 8With the large and smallest round cutters, cut the dough into the unique round shape. If you dont have cutters, you could use a small round bowl and a plastic bottle cover.keep about two of the midle cutouts, you'd need those later to test the oil. for jam doughnuts, you'd need just the larger cutter or bowl. Once you've cut the doughnuts, leave to rise in a warm place for about 30mins.
  • STEP 9In a deep fryer or small pot with dept, heat the oil till hot, you can test if the oil is ready by dropping one of the small cutouts into the hot oil. If it rises to the surface then it's ready. Deep fry the doughnuts till golden brown, making sure you don't crowd the fryer.
  • STEP 10Remove from the fryer and drain on a paper towel.
  • STEP 11Sprinkle with sugar, fill with jam or syrup, Dip in incing sugar and sprinkle with colored candies and serve with cold or hot beverage.

BUNS

30 mins | 500 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • kg of all purpose four
  • of tablespoon of butter
  • tablespoon of baking powder
  • kg of sugar
  • ml of Water
  • egg
  • Vanilla extract(Optional)
  • kg Vegetable oil for frying

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Add all the dry ingredients with the butter in a bowl and mix to form crumble.
  • STEP 2 Add the egg and water and mix to form a thick stretchy batter, then add water to the flour in bits tll you achieve the desired consistency. Do not overwork the batter.
  • STEP 3 Place a shallow pan on medium heat, add vegetable oil and allow to heat up till hot.
  • STEP 4 By dropping in a small batter, test the oil for readiness. If the batter floats atop, then it's ready.
  • STEP 5 Carefully drop the batter in hot oil in batches but don't overcrowd the oil
  • STEP 6Reduce the heatand fry the buns till golden brown and well done.
  • STEP 7Repeat this process until you have exhausted the whole batter mix
  • STEP 8Use a slotted spoon and transfer the buns on a paper towel.
  • STEP 9Sweeten to taste with sugar, refrigerate and serve chilled.

MEATPIE

30 mins | 880g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • cup of butter
  • tablespoon of Nutmeg
  • tablespoon of baking powder
  • tablespoon salt
  • big ripe pineapple
  • Water
  • Egg
  • cups of wheat flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • tablespoon of Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Habanero minced
  • lb ground beef
  • Tablespoon thyme
  • tablespoon Olive oil
  • Potatoes diced
  • Carrots diced
  • Corn Starch

METHOD

  • STEP 1 In a large pan, preheat the oil then add the diced Onion. Stir-fry till the Onions becomes translucent and limp.
  • STEP 2 Add the minced Habanero pepper to give it some heat and aroma then you stir-fry for a few seconds. Then add the ground beef. Try to break the meat apart as you don't want clumps forming in the filling.
  • STEP 3 Once the beef is well browned, add your spices and the diced potatoes plus enough water to cook the potatoes till it becomes tender.
  • STEP 5 Stir in the diced carrot and cook again for another 5 minutes. Add the cornstarch and water mixture to slightly thicken up the filling and leave to simmer for about 2-3 mins.
  • STEP 6 Take it off the heat and let it cool down while you prepare the pastry dough.
  • STEP 7 In a large bowl, bring together flour, baking powder, nutmeg, salt, brown sugar, and whisk together flour, baking powder, nutmeg, salt, brown sugar and whisk together.
  • STEP 8 Add the cubed butter or margarine, to the dry ingredients and work the butter/Margarine into the flour with your hands until it becomes crumbly.
  • STEP 9 Add the Egg and mix it into the flour until it is well incorporated, then, begin to introduce the cold water a little at a time and use your hands to toss the flour into th water until it's fully absorbed. Once the dough is formed, cover it up with a plastic wrap and put it in the fridge for at least 20 mins.
  • STEP 10 After 20 mins, take the dough out from the fridge, and roll it out on a lightly floured work surface. Then use the medium size pie cut out your circles. If you happen to not have a pie cutter, you can use any circular object around the house to cut out your dough
  • STEP 11 Remove the pie from the pie cutter and pace it on a parchment lined baking sheet.
  • STEP 12 Place the pies in a 350 degrees F preheated oven and bake for 25 - 20mins till the surface of the pie becomes golden.

SPRING ROLL

30 minutes | 250 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • - pounds of beef
  • - tablespoon of your choice
  • - onion small diced
  • - scallion diced
  • - stock cube
  • - tablespoon chili pepper
  • - tablespoon black pepper
  • - 3 cup cabbage shredded
  • - cup carrots shredded and curry powder
  • - Water
  • - salt to taste
  • - Enough Oil to fry

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Preheat the oil in the pan and saute the onion and scallion until it's translucent then add the chill pepper, black pepper, curry powder, salt to taste and the stock cube.
  • STEP 2 Add the ground beef and use a wooden spoon or spatula to break it up into tiny pieces then stir fry until the meat is cooked through then add the shredded cabbage and carrots and turn off the heat to prevent the vegetable from turning soggy and leave it to cool before using it.
  • STEP 3 In a bowl, mix together the flour and water to make the paste and set that aside
  • STEP 4 RAdd about 1 tablespoon of filing to the spring roll sheet you can add a little more depending on the size of your sheets but don't ovefill it, else, it's going to burst while frying it, then secure it with the paste and roll it up .
  • STEP 5 Fry in a preheated oil of about 350 degrees F until golden brown. Serve with your favourite dip or sauce or eat as is, Enjoy!

Plantain Chips

20 minutes | 1000 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • - Plantains large
  • - Salt to taste
  • - Enough Oil to fry
  • - Black pepper to taste

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Cut off both ends of the plantain hen run your knife through the flesh of the plantain creating a cut from the top to the bottom. Try not to cu too deep into the flesh, you just want to cut through the skin.
  • STEP 2 Using a mandoline slicer, slice plantains into long, thin stripes. if the plantain is too long, divide it into two before slicing it.
  • STEP 3 Put the cut plantain in alarge bowl, and season with salt and black pepper.
  • STEP 4 Preheat some Oil sufficient enough to deep fry he plantain. Add the plantains and fry them stirring continuosly until they become golden and cripsy.
