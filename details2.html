SOUP

AFANG

30 mins | 900 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • kg of beef or assorted meat(cow skin, shaki(tripe) etc)
  • grams of smoked fish, stock fish
  • spoons of palm oil
  • handful shalled periwinkle(option)
  • tablespoonful ground crayfish
  • kg of Afang leave(same as okazi/ukazi leaves)
  • kg of water leave(or lamb lettuce or spinach as an alternative if you live outside Africa)
  • Fresh pepper (scotch bonnet/atarodo) or ground dried pepper(to taste)
  • kg Snails
  • grams of Crayfish
  • Maggi/ Salt to taste

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Slice the water leaves and set aside.
  • STEP 2 Afang leaves, pound or blend it and set aside.
  • STEP 3 Cook the assorted meat and stock fish with chopped onions, one stock fish with chopped onions, one cube of maggi and salt to taste.Cook until tender, then clean the dried fish in hot water, remove the bones and add the fish to the pot of cooked meat.
  • STEP 4 Chop / slice, then blend the Ewedu, add it to a small pot of boiling water with a bit of Potassium salt (Kanhu) to soften, include the locust beans, crayfish and a pinch of salt to taste. Cook the Ewedu until it feels slippery or draws.
  • STEP 5 Add the palm oil, grounded pepper ,crayfish and remaining stock cube to the pot. Mix well and then add periwinkles(if you are using any).
  • STEP 6 Cover the pot and leave to boil for about 10 mins.
  • STEP 7 After 10 mins, add the Afang leaves, leave to simmer for 3 to 5 mins and then add the water leaves. Crayfish(as you like it).
  • STEP 8If you are using fresh Afang leaves, let it simmer for 3 mins. Add salt to taste, simmer for 2 mins

EFO RIRO

30 mins | 880g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • or two bunches of Spinach Vegetable / Efo tete.
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Red Chilli Peppers
  • Blended pepper with onions.
  • Smoked Stock Fish.
  • Periwinkle.
  • Maggi/Salt or other seasoning.
  • Dry Stock Fish.
  • Boiled Seasoned Crab / Snails / Shrimps
  • Boiled and seasoned assorted Meat (Beef parts)
  • Wooden spatula for stirring
  • spoonful of Palm oil

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Fry four cooking spoons of palm oil in a deep cooking pan, slice onions and add salt, then leave for 2mins.
  • STEP 2 Add the blended pepper, small red chilli pepper, ½ a clove of chopped garlic and ginger and cook for 10 minutes.
  • STEP 3 Add all the ingredients, sliced tomatoes , boiled beef, Cray fish, stock dry fish, assorted meat, shrimps, crab, snail and seasoned, add periwinkle.
  • STEP 4 Add the Spinach vegetable and cook for a further 2-3mins stir together.

BANGA

1 Hour | 600 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • oz Palm kernel nuts
  • Onions
  • pieces Pepper (yellow pepper)
  • Seasoning (Maggi, Tyko, Rogojie, Obeletientien)
  • kg Beef meat/goat meat/fish
  • oz Periwinkle
  • stick Oburunbebe

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Boil palm kernel nut for 25mins.
  • STEP 2 Check the palm kernel if soften, pour out water and extract the oil by pounding the nut with a mortar, after which, add some water.
  • STEP 3 Put the extract in a pot and boil for 30 minutes.
  • STEP 4 Pound the pepper, tyko, rogojie, sprinkle few leaves of obeletientien, add all to the soup, add a pinch of native salt and seasoning, break the oburun-bebe stick into 2 and drop ½ of the stick in the soup, these spices gives it a great aroma and taste.
  • STEP 5 Add the meats/fish/beef, snail, periwinkle and boil for 15mins, after this, check seasoning if tasty. The soup becomes thick. Remove the oburunbebe (brown stick). Save for another use.
  • STEP 5 Serve the soup in with Fufu, starch, even Eba.
  • NOTE When warming the soup, warm in a native plate. If unable to get these spices (tyko, rogojie, obletientien, oburunbebe), cook the soup without them and add Pomo, stock fish, snails, allow cooking for 35mins.

EWEDU

20 minutes | 700 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • - cups of jute leaves.
  • - Tablespoons locust beans(Iru).
  • - stock cubes
  • - salt to taste.
  • - Maggi/or other seasoning to taste.

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Pick the jute leaves from the stalk and wash with water to get rid of dirt.
  • STEP 2 Put in a blender and blend with little water.
  • STEP 3 Pour the blended ewedu in a pot
  • STEP 4 Add locust beans, stock cubes and salt. put on heat and cook for 2 mins.
  • STEP 5 Take off heat and that's it!!!

EGUSI

25 minutes | 250 g Nutrition | Serving

INGREDIENTS

  • - cup blended onions(about 3-5 and fresh chilies, to taste)
  • - cups of Egusi(melon seeds, grounded or milled).
  • - cup of palm oil.
  • - teaspoons fresh iru(locust beans).
  • - Grounded crayfish(to taste).
  • - Boiled and seasoned assorted Meat (Beef parts).
  • - Smoked or Stock Fish.
  • - Dry Stock Fish.
  • - cup of waterleaf(cut)
  • - Bitter leaf(to taste)
  • - Periwinkle.
  • - Maggi/Salt or other seasoning.

METHOD

  • STEP 1 Prepare the egusi paste.
  • STEP 2 Blend egusi seeds and onion mixture. Set aside
  • STEP 3 In a large pot, heat the palm oil on medium for a minute and then add the Iru
  • STEP 4Slowly add the stock and set on low heat to simmer
  • STEP 5Scoop teaspoon size balls of the egusi paste mixture into the stock. Be sure to keep ball shape.
  • STEP 6Leave to simmer for 20-30 mins so the balls cook through.
  • STEP 7Add the meat and fish and other bits which you'd like to use.
  • STEP 8Add the waterleaf
  • STEP 9Stir and put a lid on the pot and allow cook for 7-10 mins, till the leaves wilt.
  • STEP 10Add the bitter leaf. Leave the lid of while the cooking finishes for another 5 - 10 mins.
  • STEP 11Stir, check seasoning and adjust accordingly.
